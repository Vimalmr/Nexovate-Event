Kongu Engineering College's Nexovate takes center stage on March 8, 2024. Mark your calendars for the showcase!

Nexovate 2024 will take place on March 8 2024,Friday. The department of Computer Science and Design in Kongu Engineering College organizes this event will converge upon the students from various colleges enthusiasm and intention to thrive from various institutions.The event begins at 8.45 AM in the IT park Ramanunja Seminar Hall and this symposium comprises to be an symposium of various genres, ensuring an enlightening experience. It includes four technical, three non-technical and two workshops. The Computer Science and Design Association and Computer Science and Design Coding Club have made necessary arrangements that includes food and transport for the participants to experience the event. There is a cash pool of "Twenty Thousand Rupees" and each event winners will get exciting cash prizes.The participants are requested to join us in prominent events and disseminate the information and knowledge with other participants.The students of KEC-CSD will also join us for this exciting event and share a nice memory with you.As The countdown of the event started, book the tickets and get on track. The stage is set, and students are ready with excitement. See you there.

About Us

Kongu Engineering College, one of the foremost multi professional research-led Institutions is internationally a recognized leader in professional and career-oriented education. It provides an integral, inter-disciplinary education - a unique intersection between theory and practice, passion and reason. The College offers courses of study that are on the frontiers of knowledge and it connects the spiritual and practical dimensions of intellectual life, in a stimulating environment that fosters rigorous scholarship and supportive community. This Institute is a great possession of the committed Trust. The noble Trust has taken the institute to greater heights and has established the college as a forum for imparting value based education.
Computer Science and Design programme aims to develop graduates with computing tools and technologies, along with the interactive design approaches and its technologies. The program will prepare students to work in the IT industry as well as in design industry like virtual/augmented reality, gaming, IoT, etc., which is used to control our environment. Good use of Interaction design principles and techniques is a very high priority for software development companies. Our vision is to be a centre of excellence for bringing up skilled computer professionals with enriched interactive design skills, high caliber and quality for catering to the dynamic needs of the industry and society.
